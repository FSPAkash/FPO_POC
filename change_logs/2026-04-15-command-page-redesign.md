# Command Page Redesign Log

Date: 2026-04-15
Scope: Presentation-focused redesign of the `Command` page to explain the agentic flow to a non-technical audience.
Workspace note: This workspace does not appear to have an active `.git` repository, so rollback support is documented here and source backups were created before editing.

## Goals

- Make the command page client-presentation worthy.
- Reframe the experience around a clear business story instead of raw operations tables.
- Show the end-to-end agentic flow in plain language.
- Preserve drill-down detail without making detail the first thing a client sees.

```powershell
Copy-Item backups\sectionViews.jsx.2026-04-15-command-page.bak frontend\src\sectionViews.jsx -Force

```

## Design Intent

- Lead with outcomes and explainability, not infrastructure.
- Show the flow as a story a client can follow in one pass.
- Keep live credibility through recent proof points and throughput.
- Keep detailed tables available, but behind secondary interactions.
