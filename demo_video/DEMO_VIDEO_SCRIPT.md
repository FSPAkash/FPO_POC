# FPO Integrated OS Demo Video Script

Target runtime: about 1 minute

## 1. Title card

Visual: branded intro slide

Voiceover:
FPO Integrated OS is an agent-led operating platform for farmer producer organizations. This walkthrough shows how the system connects registry, fulfillment, market execution, communication, approvals, and carbon readiness in one operating layer.

## 2. Login and product promise

Visual: login screen

Voiceover:
The product opens with a simple promise: five agents, one command center. The goal is autonomous by default, with human intervention only where confidence or policy says it is needed.

## 3. Command Center

Visual: command center

Voiceover:
The command center gives managers a live view of the autonomous state. You can see active queues, recent agent runs, work done automatically, and the exact items that still need people.

## 4. Agentic Flow Walkthrough

Visual: flow walkthrough

Voiceover:
This screen makes the message-to-execution model easy to explain. Farmer intake routes the signal, fulfillment and crop-cycle agents act, market allocation converts supply into business execution, and staff only step in by exception.

## 5. Farmer Network

Visual: farmer network

Voiceover:
The farmer network is the shared source of truth for FPOs, farmers, villages, plots, seasons, and communication profiles. Every downstream workflow runs on this operating data.

## 6. Fulfillment

Visual: operations / fulfillment tracker

Voiceover:
Fulfillment is where input demand becomes execution. The system captures farmer asks, groups compatible demand, raises procurement, updates inventory, tracks collections, and keeps settlements connected to the same flow.

## 7. Market Execution

Visual: market execution

Voiceover:
Once produce is collected, market execution shows the best-fit buyer demand, creates sales orders, and tracks dispatch follow-through so value realization is not lost after the deal is made.

## 8. Human Handoff Desk

Visual: handoff desk with floating phone

Voiceover:
When confidence is low or judgement is required, work lands in the human handoff desk. The floating farmer phone shows the farmer-side conversation that triggered the exception, so office teams always see the business context.

## 9. Campaigns and Outreach

Visual: campaigns

Voiceover:
The same system also handles advisories, inbox traffic, and outbound campaigns. That means proactive crop-cycle outreach and reactive support can live in one communication layer.

## 10. Approvals and Audit

Visual: approvals

Voiceover:
This is where governed autonomy becomes practical. Thresholds route purchase requests and other sensitive actions into a single approval desk, with a clear audit trail behind every agent and human decision.

## 11. Carbon Readiness

Visual: carbon

Voiceover:
Carbon readiness sits on top of the same data spine. Because the system already captures plots, crops, and practice records, climate workflows can be layered in without creating another disconnected tool.

## 12. Closing card

Visual: closing slide

Voiceover:
Taken together, the platform gives FPOs one operating system instead of many disconnected workflows: autonomous by default, human only by exception.
